Pickup • Local Delivery • Mail Shipping

Delivery & Shipping Options

Pickup: Pickup details are provided after your order is confirmed.

Local delivery: Available in the North Charleston area. Enter your address during checkout to calculate availability and delivery fee.

Mail shipping: Available for eligible baked goods only. Shipping is quoted separately after your order is reviewed and packed.

Important Shipping Notice

Cheesecake items cannot be shipped.

Cheesecake treats are available for pickup or local delivery only so they stay fresh and safe to enjoy.

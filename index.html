Delivery & Shipping Options

Pickup: Pickup details are provided after your order is confirmed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you deliver?

Yes. Local delivery is available in the North Charleston area. Enter your address during checkout to check availability.

Can cheesecake be shipped?

No. Cheesecake items are available for pickup or local delivery only.

How much notice do custom orders need?

Most custom orders require at least 48 hours' notice. Larger orders may need more time.

How will I know my order is ready?

You will receive email updates when your order is received, preparing, ready, shipped, or delivered.

Do you ship nationwide?

Shipping is available for eligible baked goods. Shipping is quoted separately after your order is reviewed and packed.

Do you handle allergies?

Please include allergy information in the notes. Some products may contain or come into contact with common allergens.
